Our Beer

Our signature beer is Two Skulls - a hop forward yet well balanced American Pale Ale.

It has taken years of development to get Two Skulls tasting as it does today. With our natural spring water from deep in the Ruahine ranges and our meticulous hygiene standards, we have used a strict scientific approach to developing this beer - first consistently producing a good brew, and then working on countless iterations of improvements.

Who are we

Our brewery started on the outskirts of the Pohangina village, deep in the mountains of rural Manawatu. Our brewers - Mike and Joe - started out their brewing journey after a yarn over a beer awakened an old passion for brewing.

From the first brew - huddled against the cold around an old pot over a basic gas stove, the brewers developed quickly by leveraging their combined Physics and Engineering knowledge.

A few years later, Outskirt Brewery has developed tremendously from that first brew. With a wall of stainless steel equipment forming our pilot micro-brewery, and ongoing collaboration with Massey University, we will see how far this little brewery can grow.
